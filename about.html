Architecting the Future

The27LINKS is not just a company; it is a convergence of pure code and raw silicon. Founded by Robert Storm, we are building the bridge between digital software and physical reality.

The Origin Story

In an era where technology is becoming increasingly closed-off and "black-boxed," The27LINKS was born out of a desire to understand, break, and rebuild.

It started as a simple idea in a B.Tech dorm room: Why choose between software and hardware? Most companies do one or the other. We believe the future belongs to those who can write the Python script and solder the circuit board it runs on.

The Master Plan

We are currently in Phase 1. Here is where we are going.

2024 - 2025: The Foundation

Software Services & Education

Establishing our digital footprint. We are focusing on delivering high-quality Python automation, Web Development services, and Educational resources for students. Status: Active.

2026: The Bridge

R&D and Prototyping

The lab opens. We begin research into IoT (Internet of Things) devices and custom security tools. Transitioning from pure code to code-that-controls-things.

2027: The Ecosystem

Consumer Electronics

Launch of "The27" line of self-designed gadgets. Affordable, open-source friendly, and powered by our own software ecosystem.
